I'm a bubble tea fan!
Besides software engineering, I have a passion for bubble tea! There's a ton of them in Vietnam, but it wasn't so easy to get one in MN. I don't have a car, and the bubble tea stores aren't close enough to walk from campus, especially in the winter. Therefore, few years ago, I would get super happy when I had a chance to go to a mall that had a bubble tea store, or when someone went somewhere and got me bubble tea on the way back. This picture was when a friend got me a surprise bubble tea on a very cold winter night that she brought all the way from Mall of America. You can't see my face but tell me if that's not a happy face right there?
